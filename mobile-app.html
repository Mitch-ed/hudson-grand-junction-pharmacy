Mobile App

Manage Your Prescriptions from Anywhere

Our upcoming mobile app is designed to make managing your medications as easy as possible. Whether you're at home, at work, or on the go, you'll be able to request refills, check prescription status, and stay organized — all without making a phone call or waiting in line.

The app will be available for iPhone and Android and will connect directly to your Hudson Grand Junction Pharmacy account. We'll post download links here as soon as it goes live.

Coming soon. Download links for the App Store and Google Play will appear here once our mobile app launches. In the meantime, give us a call at 731-433-4500 for refills, transfers, or any other prescription questions.

Features

Everything You Need, Built In

Our upcoming app is being designed with patients in mind — straightforward, not complex. Here's what to look forward to.

One-Tap Refills

Request a refill on any of your active prescriptions with a single tap. We'll notify you when it's ready for pickup.

Prescription History

View all of your current and past prescriptions in one organized list. Great for keeping track of what you take and when.

Refill Reminders

Set automatic reminders so you never run out of a medication. The app can alert you a few days before you're due to run low.

Pickup Notifications

Get notified as soon as your prescription is filled and ready. No more guessing — you'll know the moment it's good to go.

Family Account Management

Manage prescriptions for your entire family from a single app account. Ideal for parents managing medications for children.

Hours & Contact

View Hudson Grand Junction Pharmacy's hours, contact info, and location directly in the app. Call or email us with one tap.

Getting Started

Here's How It Will Work

Once our mobile app launches, getting set up will be quick and easy. You won't need any technical knowledge — just a smartphone and a few minutes.

1

Download the App

Once it's released, you'll be able to find our app in the App Store or Google Play. We'll post direct download links here as soon as it's live.

2

Create Your Account

Register with your name, date of birth, and the phone number on file at Hudson Grand Junction Pharmacy. Your prescriptions will sync automatically.

3

Manage Your Medications

Request refills, set reminders, and view your prescription history — all from the app. It's that simple.

4

Pick Up In Store

We'll send a notification when your prescription is ready. Swing by during store hours and you're all set.
